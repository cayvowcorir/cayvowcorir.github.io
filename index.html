Kiangunyi Girls High School Website

Kiangunyi Girls High School is a school located in Kenya. Together with the team at my company Hirazi Softwares LLC, we made their desire for online presence become a reality.

Motivation

To make the school's service delivery easier

Leaving a smile on the faces of the students of Kiangunyi Girls High school and their parents as they enjoy services being offered online was the driving force behind the project. Achieving that brought the greatest satisfaction.

The site is currently up and running, and is serviced and maintained by Hirazi Softwares, with a lifetime service guarantee. All the components are working as intended, and the purpose was achieved, so to say.

The interface

The site's interface is fully responsive. Anyone who accesses the site will be amazed at the ease of use and it's fluidity.

  • Informative Graphs

    Graphs have been integrated into the site that offer an overview of the students' performance.

  • Interactive Forms

    The site is made using Bootstrap and the forms are interactive and easy to fill. Not the kind you would get bored with

  • Photo Gallery

    An amazing photo gallery of the school, well designed and well displayed.
