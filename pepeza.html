This is an application (Patent pending) conceptualized by My Team and I that allows you to subscribe to noticeboards, post and view notices. You do not have to keep track of notices on the physical noticeboards around you. Visualize a scenario where there are dozens of notices you have to keep track of, and they are all distributed over a wide area. It would be quite hectic to visit all the noticeboards and keep track of each new notice. That is where Pepeza steps in...
